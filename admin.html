How to get the response sheet URL

  1. Open the form in Google Forms (use the form links above).
  2. Go to the Responses tab.
  3. Click the green Sheets icon (Link to Sheets) → “Create a new spreadsheet” or “Select existing spreadsheet”.
  4. Open that spreadsheet and copy the URL from the browser (e.g. https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/ABC123.../edit).
  5. Paste it into the box above and click “Open sheet” to jump to it anytime.

Your sheet URLs are saved in this browser only (localStorage).
